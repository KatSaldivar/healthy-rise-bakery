About Healthy Rise Bakery

Mary is a Registered Dietitian and member of Bread Bakers Guild of America who thinks food can be satisfying and healthy without being supersized or loaded with calories and fat. She bakes using organic ingredients mostly from local sources. Our bakers start work before the sun rises each day to ensure that every batch is as fresh as possible before it gets to you. We're dedicated to kickstarting your morning with warm muffins, rolls and bread and gluten free desserts. We buy local and grind our own flour for every batch. We love supplying local coffee shops/cafes and working with businesses to deliver fresh bread, rolls, muffins and gluten free muffins and cookies to your employees for greater employee engagement.
